# 📱 SOCIAL MEDIA STRATEGIE - 4 Seasons

## Platform: Instagram & Facebook

### Content Pillars (Wekelijks)

**PILLAR 1: VOOR/NA FOTO'S (50%)**
- Split-screen: Links smerig → rechts kristalhelder
- Caption: "Dit was maandag. Dinsdag perfect. #4SeasonsClean"
- Post rhythm: 2x per week (Monday, Friday)
- Expected engagement: 8-12% (high trust-builder)

**PILLAR 2: TEAM & PROCESS (30%)**
- Behind-the-scenes video: Team aan het werk
- Close-ups van tools, technieken, attention-to-detail
- Caption: "Dit doen wij liever dan praten."
- Post rhythm: 1x per week (Wednesday)

**PILLAR 3: TESTIMONIALS (15%)**
- Client success stories (video or quote + photo)
- Real names, real faces, real reviews
- Caption: Name + testimonial (max 150 chars)
- Post rhythm: 1x per week (Thursday)

**PILLAR 4: EDUCATIE (5%)**
- "Tip van de week" - How to maintain clean windows
- "Welke reinigingsmiddelen gebruiken?" - Q&A format
- Post rhythm: 2x per month (Tuesday)

### Analytics & KPIs

Track on Instagram/Facebook Business:
- 📊 Save rate (shares > likes → high-value content)
- 💬 Comment rate (>3% = good engagement)
- 🔗 Click-through to website (track UTM: ?utm_source=instagram)
- 🎬 Video view rate (aim: 60%+ completion)

---

### Visual Branding

- **Color Palette:** Blue (#2563EB) + Green (#22C55E) + White
- **Filter:** Cool, bright, natural lighting (NOT oversaturated)
- **Font:** Sans-serif, readable (use stickers, not heavy overlays)
- **Aspect Ratio:** 
  - Posts: 1.091:1 (Instagram square)
  - Stories: 9:16 (full screen)
  - Reels: 9:16 (video)
